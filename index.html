Welcome to my page!

This is an introduction page to two of my nearest and dearest OCs. In my spare time, I love creating stories for these characters I draw and bringing them to life. The two featured on this website are two of my most loved OCs, Ethan and Raiden. I occasionally stream in my freetime, and Raiden is actually my PNGtuber that I drew myself. Ethan was the base for my VTuber I have as well, though that one I did not draw myself.

These two pages will give you an introduction to these two OCs, letting you understand more of their character while also absorbing what they look like with attached images.
